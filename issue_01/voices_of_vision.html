Voices of Vision

Interviews

The interviews in Asian Creatives Collective highlight the voices shaping the creative world today. From established names to rising talents, these conversations dive into the stories behind the work, the inspirations driving innovation, and the cultural roots that tie everything together.

For the first issue, the editor and creator of the publication personally conducted each interview and thoughtfully curated this selection of interviewees. These creatives were chosen not only for their exceptional talent but also for their ability to inspire and challenge the narratives surrounding Asian identity and artistry.

Each conversation offers a glimpse into the creative process, the intersection of heritage and modernity, and the shared journey of redefining what it means to be an Asian creative in today’s world. Together, these stories serve as a celebration of resilience, innovation, and cultural pride, setting the stage for a new era of artistic expression.

Flip through and meet the voices driving a dynamic and ever-evolving global creative community.

08 November 2024

Sai Vaishnavi Ramachandran/Niva Shah

Graphic Designer

Sai Vaishnavi Ramachandran is a junior at Parsons School of Design, pursuing a degree in Communication Design. Originally from Bangalore, India, and now based in New York City, Sai is a multidisciplinary designer with a passion for translating ideas into impactful visuals. Specializing in motion graphics, creative coding, and print, Sai seamlessly blends traditional and digital mediums to craft compelling narratives.

With a focus on innovative design solutions, Sai enjoys exploring the intersection of technology and creativity. Whether coding dynamic visuals, animating striking motion pieces, or crafting tactile print designs, Sai brings concepts to life with a unique perspective. Driven by curiosity and a love for experimentation, Sai continues to push boundaries and create meaningful work that resonates across mediums and audiences.


07 November 2024

Neha Marina/Niva Shah

Graphic Designer

Neha Marina is a junior at Parsons School of Design, majoring in Communication Design with a minor in Printmaking. A versatile designer, Neha combines a strong foundation in traditional design with expertise in web design, data visualization, and printmaking. She is passionate about creating work that is not only visually engaging but also functional and impactful.

With a keen eye for detail and a problem-solving mindset, Neha excels in responsive design, coding, and crafting seamless user experiences. Her dedication to intentionality and precision is evident in everything she creates, whether it’s through digital platforms or print media. Beyond her technical skills, Neha values collaboration and the ability to connect with diverse audiences. As a designer, she strives to produce meaningful work that bridges the gap between creativity and innovation.


10 November 2024

Jacqueline Pramana/Niva Shah

Founder of Aqura

Jacqueline Pramana is a junior at Parsons School of Design, majoring in Communication Design. Based in New York City, she is a passionate graphic designer specializing in branding, typography, and merchandising, with a focus on creating impactful and intentional designs.

Jacqueline is also the founder of Aqura, an Asian-owned brand based in NYC that crafts unique, handmade jewelry from shells and beads. Combining her design expertise with her entrepreneurial spirit, she creates pieces that celebrate individuality and natural beauty. Through Aqura, Jacqueline merges artistry and craftsmanship, reflecting her dedication to thoughtful storytelling and cultural expression. As both a designer and entrepreneur, she continues to create work that resonates with diverse audiences while honoring her creative vision and heritage.


07 November 2024

Aashriya Dinesh/Niva Shah

Co-Founder of Neptune-House

Aashriya Dinesh is a sophomore Strategic Design major at Parsons School of Design, blending her fine arts background with a passion for creative strategy and storytelling. As the co-founder of Neptune House NYC, a multidisciplinary art collective, she creates platforms that connect students with professional opportunities through curated showcases and networking events. Her work reflects her commitment to fostering emerging talent while emphasizing the values of integrity, quality, and intentionality.

Passionate about preservation, marketing, and impactful collaborations, Aashriya approaches every project with a critical and innovative perspective. Whether it’s developing campaigns, organizing events, or activating brands, she strives to build meaningful projects that resonate deeply. With her entrepreneurial drive and dedication to bridging the gap between creativity and professional growth, Aashriya is shaping a new standard for collaboration in the creative industry.


10 November 2024

Reinhardt Kenneth/Niva Shah

Fashion Designer

Reinhardt Kenneth is an Indonesian fashion and celebrity photographer based in Los Angeles, CA. Discovered at 14 by the late Franca Sozzani, the legendary Editor-in-Chief of Vogue Italia, his work has been featured in Vogue Italia, The Louvre in Paris, Art Basel Miami, and exhibitions in New York, Cinque Terre, and Indonesia. With a portfolio spanning campaigns, billboards, magazine covers, and global celebrities, Reinhardt has become a visionary force in the industry.

Known for his bold, cinematic style, Reinhardt approaches each project with a meticulously crafted vision, collaborating with his creative team to bring concepts to life. His work transcends traditional photography, blending art, fashion, and emotion into striking visuals that celebrate individuality and innovation.


10 November 2024

Bach Mai/Niva Shah

Fashion Designer

Bach Mai is a visionary Vietnamese-American designer redefining the boundaries of luxury fashion. Born and raised in Houston, Texas, he draws deeply from his cultural heritage to craft designs that blend couture-level craftsmanship with modern innovation. A graduate of Parsons School of Design and the Institut Français de la Mode, Bach honed his skills at iconic houses like Oscar de la Renta, Calvin Klein, and under the mentorship of John Galliano at Maison Margiela.

Since launching his eponymous label, Bach has captivated the fashion world with his signature aesthetic of “irreverent glamour and unabashed femininity,” seamlessly marrying opulence with accessibility. His partnership with renowned fabric house Hurel further cements his dedication to quality and artistry. With a focus on empowering clients and celebrating individuality, Bach Mai is a rising force in contemporary couture, making his mark globally while staying true to his roots.


10 November 2024

Liza Koshy/Niva Shah

Actor

Liza Koshy is an influential Indian-American entertainer redefining comedy, storytelling, and representation in the creative industry. She first rose to fame on Vine and YouTube, captivating audiences with her sharp humor and infectious personality. Transitioning into acting, producing, and hosting, Liza has become a global icon, starring in hit projects like Liza on Demand and Work It while hosting major events, including the Met Gala red carpet and Double Dare.

Her creativity and versatility have earned her multiple Streamy and Teen Choice Awards, showcasing her impact across entertainment. Beyond her career, Liza uses her platform to advocate for mental health awareness, inclusivity, and social progress, empowering others to embrace their authentic selves. As a trailblazer in the industry, she continues to inspire and pave the way for a new generation of Asian creatives making their mark.
